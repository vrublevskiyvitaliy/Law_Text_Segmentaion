ASSET PURCHASE AGREEMENT
This Asset Purchase Agreement (this "Agreement") is made and entered into as of December __, 2009, by and among DUTCH GOLD RESOURCES, INC., a publicly-owned Nevada Corporation, (Dutch Gold), DGRI AGDI ACQUISITION CORPORATION, a Georgia corporation (the Purchaser), and AULTRA GOLD, INC., a an publicly-owned Nevada corporation (the Seller).
Dutch Gold, the Purchaser and the Seller are referred to herein individually as a Party and collectively as the Parties.

RECITALS
WHEREAS, the Seller is in the business of acquiring and exploring gold and mineral properties and is the owner of certain assets of gold and precious mineral properties and certain assets and equipment related to the exploration and development of such properties (the Mining Business);
WHEREAS, the Purchaser is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Dutch Gold; and
WHEREAS, the Seller desires to sell and Purchaser desires to purchase all of the Sellers right, title and interest in the Mining Business and the related assets and services as provided herein, pursuant to the terms and conditions set forth herein;
NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the premises and the mutual promises made in this Agreement, and for other good and valuable consideration, the sufficiency and receipt of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties agree as follows.
