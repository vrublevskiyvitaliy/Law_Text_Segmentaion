  • 1. Both parties confirm that as of December 5, 2008, Party B has supplied 38,763.

  • 4. The place of delivery shall be the warehouse in Party Bs factory

  • 5. Upon execution of this Supplementary Agreement, both parties do not have any other disputes regarding performance of the Original Contract (Contract No.
