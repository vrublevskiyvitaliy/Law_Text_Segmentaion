          • (i) if the Applicable Market Value is greater than or equal to $5.
          • (ii) if the Applicable Market Value is less than the Threshold Appreciation Price but greater than $4.
          • (iii) if the Applicable Market Value is less than or equal to the Initial Price, then the Conversion Rate will be 5.4348 shares of Common Stock per Note (the Maximum Conversion Rate).
